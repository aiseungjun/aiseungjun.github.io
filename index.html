Research

My research goal is to enhance diagnostic capabilities by integrating AI into various medical scenarios. And, I'm interested in computer vision, deep Learning for Medical Application, generative AI, 3D, and development of AI-based applications. Toward this goal, I am focused on developing medical imaging models, particularly for CT denoising or reconstruction, incorporating the latest models and techniques such as RLHF, while adopting a medical-based approach.

Some research lacks code or does not include it due to confidentiality or because the code is not yet tidied up. However, in second case, it will be made available soon.
